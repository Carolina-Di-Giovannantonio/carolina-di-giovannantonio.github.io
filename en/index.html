Carolina Di Giovannantonio

Hello! I’m Carolina Di Giovannantonio, graphic design student in constant exploration. In the first years of my career I learnt to observe the space surrounding me. This webiste presents some projects I have made in these three years.

editorial planet

superhero cards

This superhero and her villain nemesis were created from an analysis of personal strenghts and weaknesses in graphic design. Clear acetate printing was used to represent transparency as a designer quality.

museums map

The museums map presents a route through the museums of Montevideo with an educational design intended for a school-age audience. Project carried out together with Magela García, Romina Justet and Fiorella Veglio.

object book

Object book. Creation of a silkscreen printed book, based on Lewis Carroll's story, Alice in Wonderland. Project carried out together with Magela García.

typography planet

Petrona Viera typography

The Petrona Viera typography is based on the woodcuts of the Uruguayan artist. Its design was created with the purpose of being used for titles and short texts.

branding planet

product brands

Based on the identity audit of Victoria’s Secret, three product brands were created. Velvet was designed for a home goods store, Secret Notes for a stationery brand, and Light Delights for a low-calorie snack brand.

Premios Graffiti branding

Branding design for the 23rd edition of the Graffiti Awards, Uruguayan music awards, based on the fusion of musical genres through sound waves and colors. The award was made in 3D using Blender.

google doodle

Google Doodle of José Pedro Varela, commemorating the 179th anniversary of his birth. The first illustration is based on a portrait of him and a vintage blackboard. When you hover over the doodle, an illustration of Varela is superimposed next to a monitor, which would be a contemporary version of him.
